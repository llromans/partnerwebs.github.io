Depression

What Should You Do?

  • Depression is a common mental disorder. It involves a depressed mood or loss of pleasure or interest in activities for long periods of time. Depression is different from regular mood changes and feelings about everyday life.

    • What causes it?

  • Depression can be caused by things like stress, feeling lonely, big life problems, or changes in the brain’s chemicals. It can also happen after sad events, long-term stress, or not getting enough support from others.

    • How to prevent it?

  • You can prevent it by getting more exercise, cutting down on alcohol, giving up smoking and eating healthily. Reading a self-help book or joining a support group are also worthwhile. They can help you gain a better understanding about what causes you to feel depressed.
