PTSD

What is PTSD?

  • PTSD, also known as Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder, is a mental health condition that can happen after someone experiences a very scary or traumatic event.

    • What causes it?

  • PTSD is caused by experiencing or seeing something very stressful or frightening, such as accidents, violence, or natural disasters. These events can have a lasting effect on a person’s mind.

    • How to prevent it?

  • PTSD cannot always be prevented, but getting support early, talking to someone you trust, and getting help from a therapist or doctor can help reduce its effects.
