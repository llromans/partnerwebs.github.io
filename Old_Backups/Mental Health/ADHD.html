ADHD

What is ADHD?

  • ADHD, also known as Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, is a condition that makes it hard for a person to focus, stay still, or control their behavior.

    • What causes it?

  • ADHD is caused by differences in brain development and brain activity, and it can also run in families (genetics). It is not caused by laziness or bad behavior.

    • How to prevent it?

  • ADHD cannot be prevented, but it can be managed by staying organized, having a routine, getting enough sleep, and getting help from a doctor, teacher, or trusted adult.
