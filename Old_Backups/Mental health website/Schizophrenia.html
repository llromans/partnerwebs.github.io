Schizophrenia

What is Schizophrenia?

  • Schizophrenia is a mental disorder that affects how a person thinks, feels, and behaves. It can cause people to have trouble telling what is real and what is not.

    • What causes it?

  • Schizophrenia can be caused by a mix of brain chemistry, genetics (family history), and stressful life situations.

    • How to prevent it?

  • Schizophrenia cannot always be prevented, but getting support early, reducing stress, and talking to a doctor or trusted adult can help manage it.
