Questions?

Lately I’ve been feeling overwhelmed and unmotivated, even with things I used to enjoy. Has anyone else experienced this? What helped you get through it?

I’ve felt this way too, and it’s really tough when even things you used to enjoy feel like a lot. What helped me was starting small—just doing one simple thing at a time to build a bit of momentum—and focusing on basics like sleep, eating regularly, and getting some fresh air. It also helped to talk to someone I trust so I wasn’t stuck in my own head, and if it keeps going or gets worse, reaching out to a therapist can really make a difference. You’re definitely not alone in this, and it can get better even if it takes time.
