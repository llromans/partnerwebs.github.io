Phobia

What is Phobia?

  • A phobia is an intense fear of a specific object, situation, or activity, even if it is not actually dangerous. People can have different types of phobias, such as fear of heights, fear of spiders, or fear of the ocean (thalassophobia). There are many more, and millions of people around the world have phobias.

    • What causes it?

  • Phobias can be caused by past scary experiences, learned behavior from others, or brain responses to fear. They can also be linked to anxiety or genetics.

    • How to prevent it?

  • You can help prevent anxiety by managing stress, getting enough sleep, talking to someone you trust about your feelings, staying active, and practicing relaxation techniques.
