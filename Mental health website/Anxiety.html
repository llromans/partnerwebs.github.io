Anxiety

What is Anxiety?

  • Anxiety is a feeling of worry or fear about something that might happen. It can make a person feel stressed, nervous, or uneasy.

    • What causes it?

  • Anxiety can be caused by stress, fear about the future, or difficult life situations. It can also happen because of brain chemistry or family history.

    • How to prevent it?

  • You can help prevent anxiety by managing stress, getting enough sleep, and talking to someone you trust about your feelings. Staying active and practicing relaxation can also help.
