Stress

What is Stress?

  • Stress is similar to anxiety, but instead it happens when a person feels pressure or is overwhelmed by problems like school, work, or life changes.

    • What causes it?

  • Stress can be caused by too much work, school pressure, family problems, or big life changes..

    • How to prevent it?

  • ou can prevent stress by taking breaks, getting enough sleep, exercising, and talking to someone you trust
