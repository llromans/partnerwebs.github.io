What Should You Do?

So What Should You Do Exactly?

  • If you feel like you might be struggling with your mental health, try talking to someone you trust, like a friend, family member, teacher, or counselor. Getting support, taking care of your health, and asking for professional help can make a big difference.

    • What If I can't?

  • If it might feel hard to talk about or do, you can start with small steps like writing down your feelings. You can ask a teacher for help, but it is usually better to talk to someone you are closest to, like a family member or a trusted friend, since family should be your first support. The important thing is atleast to try to talk to someone instead of keeping everything to yourself.

    • Extra helpful tips

  • “Try to keep a healthy routine by getting enough sleep, staying active, eating well, and doing activities you enjoy, like hanging out with your friends, playing sports, or going out more. Doing these things can help you become more confident and feel more comfortable talking to other people and build confidence in general.
