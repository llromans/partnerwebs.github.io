Dementia

What is Dementia?

  • Dementia is a condition that affects memory, thinking, and daily activities. It mostly happens in older adults and can make it hard to remember things or make decisions.

    • What causes it?

  • Dementia is caused by damage to brain cells and can be linked to diseases like Alzheimer’s. Age, genetics, and health conditions can also increase the risk.

    • How to prevent it?

  • Dementia cannot always be prevented, but staying mentally active, eating healthy, exercising, and keeping your brain engaged can help lower the risk.
